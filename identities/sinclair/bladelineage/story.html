Sinclair (Blade Lineage Salsu)
Which side could be the real me…
The child was afraid of himself.
He couldn’t control his own shifts, and he lacked the courage to decide for himself.
“What if he really was just a crazed murderer?” Such thoughts befuddled and clouded the child’s head.
The people this child met among the Blade Lineage praised his tendencies as the mentality of a true slayer…
But those compliments didn’t do him much good when he himself doesn’t want to be like that.
Rather than inspire confidence, they only serve to burden him.
Thus, he still struggles to find his place between the pleasure of bloodshed and the unease it makes him feel.

The moon is so bright…
Expelling a breath, the child looked up into the sky.
Gazing at the moon in solitude was the one thing that could bring him comfort.
