Shi Association South Section 5 Director

The child had always expressed her joy with hearty laughs and enjoyed silly jests more than anyone, but not now.
The child’s true nature wasn’t that bright exterior.
Perhaps treating others with kind smiles and friendship was how she chose to ease her job of taking lives.
The child’s blade is swift. In the blink of an eye, before any can notice, her sword has already bisected its victim.
No expression colors the child’s face in the midst of it all.
No sorrow, no joy, no smiles nor tears.
But, when the child steps forward to meet her colleagues, her face will be painted.
Cracking jokes and laughing like there’s nary a woe.
I doubt anyone knows.
What this child… is like when she’s taking on jobs alone.
